I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Wayne State University (WSU) under the supervision of Dr. Amiangshu Bosu in Software Engineering Analytics Lab (SEAL) . I received my BSc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET), Bangladesh. My research interest lies on the area of Software Engineering, Natural Language Processing. Currently, I am working on the toxic content analysis in the Software Developers communication.
